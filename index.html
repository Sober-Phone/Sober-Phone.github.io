SOBER PHONE

Device that assists users in overcoming addictions to pornography and social media while granting them control over their social media usage, promoting moderation and healthier habits.

Why Sober Phone ?

Our Mission

The goal is to purify technology by filtering out all the distractions and explicit content. Let's transform it into a tool for knowledge, creativity, and productivity, rather than indulging in inappropriate entertainment.

The internet is flooded with explicit content that is detrimental to the well-being of today's youth. Unfortunately, avoiding it voluntarily is challenging. However, Sober phone takes a proactive approach by aggressively blocking explicit content by default.
